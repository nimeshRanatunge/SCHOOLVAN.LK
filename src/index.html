What is SURAKSHA MAGA® ?

Suraksha maga started as a solutions for bus drivers and parents both are facing problems,The software has grown and changed based on feedback from customers.The application which would use the emerging technology to help school bus fleets transport students with greater safety and efficiency.

Suraksha Maga.could deliver same real time vehicle tracking information SMS and push notifications will notify parents when the bus is just 01 kilometres away. Students get to the bus on time, without the need to stand outside for extended periods of time on dark.

  • SMS will notify when the bus arrives to the school

  • SMS will notify when the school kid not in the bus

  • SMS will notify when the bus arrives to near by Home
